## FAQ

**Q1: Why does it say connected but nothing loads?**  
A: Most often DNS is broken or traffic is blocked by firewall rules. Switch protocol, reset DNS to a known resolver, then reconnect.

**Q2: Why does the VPN work on Wi‑Fi but not on mobile hotspot (or vice versa)?**  
A: Different networks enforce different filtering/NAT behavior. Try another protocol and a closer server. Keep **Kill Switch** on to avoid partial routing.

**Q3: Can a VPN be blocked by an ISP or network admin?**  
A: Yes. Some networks throttle or block specific VPN protocols/ports. Rotate protocols/ports and test multiple **Global Servers**.

**Q4: Will turning off IPv6 help when online vpn not working?**  
A: Sometimes. IPv6 can cause split routing or DNS weirdness on certain setups. Disable IPv6 temporarily to confirm, then apply a permanent fix if needed.
